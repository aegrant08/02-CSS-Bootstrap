Welcome to My Portfolio!

My name is Andrea Grant (Andie for short). I currently work as an administrative assistant at a public accounting firm in Grand Rapids, MI. I’m hard at work learning all I can about coding to become a successful full stack web developer. I’ve worked as an administrative assistant for five years in three different industries. After a lot of research, I decided to take the leap and pursue a career in web development to achieve my dream of working as a freelancer with nothing but my laptop and a wifi signal. I attended Aquinas College and have a Bachelor of Arts degree in Liberal Arts.

When I’m not tied to my computer, I love to research the latest discoveries in classical archaeology, read a good science fiction novel, and attend Irish dance classes with friends. I truly enjoy stepping out of my comfort zone and challenging myself to try new things.
