About Me

My name is Andrea, but to my family and friends I’m known as Andie. After five years of working as an administrative assistant, I decided to take the leap and pursue a career in web development to achieve my dream of working as a freelancer with nothing but my laptop and a wifi signal. When I’m not tied to my computer, I love to research the latest discoveries in classical archaeology, read a good science fiction novel, and attend Irish dance classes with friends. Slainte!
